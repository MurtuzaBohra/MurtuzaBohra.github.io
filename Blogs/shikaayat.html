Shikaayat

By: Murtuza Bohra

Puchhta hu sabko ek mazhab kyu chunna hai.

Agar rab chahta hai insaan ibaadat kare,

toh ibaadat na karne ki wajah kyu deta hai.

Uska rab lag mera alag to mohabbat ki ijazat kyu deta hai

Aur agar ijazat di hai to usse anjaam kyu nahi deta hai.

Shikaayat hai mujhe rab se, mujhe meri mohabbat kyu nahi deta hai.
