Agar Socha Jae...

By: Murtuza Bohra

Date: Sept/2020

Agar har khuab pura ho jae,
Toh khuab aur hakeekat m fark hi kya reh jae,
Agar jise chaaha wahi chhut jae,
Toh dil ka jism se rishta hi kya reh jae.
Agar ishq haalat se ladne ko bebas ho jae,
Toh dilo k rishte aur bhi mazboot ho jae,
Agar samaajik deeware khatam ho jae,
Toh bekhof mohabbat karna mumkeen ho jae
Agar jiska saath chaha tha wo mil jae,
Toh zindagi bhar ka safar, mano chand palo mei simat jaae
Agar har adhuri khwahish puri ho jae,
Toh zindagi jeene ka maksad hi kya reh jae,
Agar madad karna ibaadat bann jae,
Toh har majboor laachar bebas ko sahara mil jae,
Agar har koi insaan bann jae,
Toh sabhi unch nich k vivaad khatam ho jae.
Agar socha hai ki kuchh haasil karliya jae,
Toh zindagi sirf sochte hue na beet jae,
Agar koshish karne mei thodi bhi chuk reh jae,
Toh marte samay tak yeh afsoos na reh jae.
