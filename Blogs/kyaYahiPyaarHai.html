Kya Yahi Pyaar Hai...

By: Murtuza Bohra

Date: Dec/2020

Ek ehsaas hai, jisme bhare boht jasbaat hai

Ek khuahish hai, jo dil k boht paas hai.

Ek ibaadat hai, jiska khuda bhi insaan hai.

Ek nashaa hai, jisse zindagi aabaad hai.

Ek zubaan hai, jisme naa lafz naa alfaaz hai.

Ek pehchaan hai, jo khud se hi anjaan hai.

Ek lamhaa hai, jisme janmo ka ehsaas hai.

Ek kitaab hai, jiska har panna ek kuraan hai.

Ek bukhar hai, jiska sab ko intazaar hai.

Ek junoon hai, jisse chalta yeh sansaar hai.

Ek Sawaal hai, jiska har dhadkan deti jawaab hai.

Kya, yahi pyaar hai?
