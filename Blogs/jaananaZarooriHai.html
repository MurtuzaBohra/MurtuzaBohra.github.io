Jaanana Zaroori Hai

Author: Murtuza Bohra

Date: Oct/2020

Kiska dosh tha, jaanana zaroori hai!!

Jab wo paida hui, parivaar tha uska, lekin fir bhi paraayi thi,
Jab padhna chahti thi, khelna chahti thi, khul kar jeena chahti thi,
Rok diya usko, daant diya, kyu ki ladki thi wo, aur ek uski hadd thi,

Kon banata hai yeh hadd jaanana zaroori hai!!

Ladka hua, ghar bhi tha parivaar bhi, aur koi hadd bhi naa thi,
Khelta tha wo, ghumta tha dosto k saath, kiseeko parwah naa thi,
Kiseene puchha tak nahi kaha tha raat bhar, lekin maa uski pareshaan thi,

Kiski beparwaahi thi jaanana zaroori hai!!

Ek din galti se nikal gayi apni hadd se bahar ki duniya mei, akeli thi,
Gahri saans li usne, dekh rahi thi idhar udhar, aur khush thi,
Ek jhund n dekha usko, wo toh anjaan thi, akeli thi, kya kar sakti thi,

Kiska jhund tha wo, jaanana zaroori hai!!

Dhund raha parivaar uska, puchhna hai sawaal usse kyu gai kaha gayi.
Dusri taraf, Ladka hai nahi aaya abhi tak, dosto k saath hoga, kiseeko koi sawal nahi,
Jab mili wo, khamosh thi, zabaan kati hui, shareer pe usk kapdo se zyada chot thi,

Kon hadd mei tha aur Kisne hadd paar ki jaanana zaroori hai!!

Galti toh thi, Sarkaar ki, samaaj ki, sanskaar ki yaa parivaar ki,
Kiska dosh tha, jaanana zaroori hai....
