Research Projects

A selection of research projects published during my academic and professional career

TextureToMTF: Predicting Spatial Frequency Response In The Wild

Having a good smartphone camera doesn't always guarantee that the images captured with it will have good quality. This research focuses on predicting spatial frequency response from texture patterns in natural images.

View Project

ColorArt: Suggesting Colorizations of Graphic Arts Using Optimal Color-Graph Matching

Graphics designs are visually appealing only when they are colored appropriately. This is a fully automatic colorization algorithm for graphic patterns using optimal color-graph matching techniques.

View Project

SmartKC: Smartphone-based Corneal Topographer for Keratoconus Detection

Keratoconus is a severe eye disease affecting the cornea, causing it to become thin and develop a conical bulge. SmartKC is a smartphone-based low-cost diagnostic tool for early detection.
